## Choosing a link shortener provider

<Step number={1} title="Open the Key System Page">
    Go to the <a href="https://www.mspaint.cc/key">Key System Page</a> and select your preferred checkpoint provider.
</Step>

<Step number={2} title="Start the Luarmor Process">
    On the Luarmor page, click the **Start** button.
</Step>

## Provider Instructions

### Through Linkvertise
<Step number={3} title="Start Linkvertise Tasks">
    Click the **Get mspaint Key** button.
</Step>

<StepImage
  src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/linkvertise/3.png"
  alt="Linkvertise get key"
/>

<Step number={4} title="Complete Required Tasks">
    <Callout type="info">
        If you receive a **one-hour wait time**, you can switch to the <a href="#through-work-ink">work.ink</a> provider instead.
    </Callout>

    Complete all required tasks, then click **Continue**.
</Step>

<StepImage
  src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/linkvertise/4.png"
  alt="Linkvertise tasks"
  width={500}
/>

<Step number={5} title="Return to Luarmor">
    After completing all required task, <a href="#final-steps">click here</a> to continue.
</Step>

---

### Through work.ink

<Step number={3} title="Start work.ink Tasks">
    Click the **Go To Destination** button.
</Step>

<StepImage
  src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/1.png"
  alt="Work.ink destination"
/>

<Step number={4} title="Continue With Ads">
    Wait, then click **Continue With Ads**.
</Step>

<StepImage
  src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/2.png"
  alt="Work.ink continue with ads"
/>

<Step number={5} title="Confirm Destination">
    Scroll down, then click **Proceed To Destination** again.
</Step>

<StepImage
  src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/3.png"
  alt="Work.ink destination confirm"
/>

<Step number={6} title="Access the Link">
    <Callout type="info">
        You **do not need to install anything**. Simply click **Install** and wait until it completes.
    </Callout>

    Wait, then click **Proceed to Safe Destination** and complete the required tasks.
</Step>

<StepImage
  src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/4.png"
  alt="Work.ink access link"
  width={700}
/>

<Step number={7} title="Complete Tasks">
    Scroll down and click **Go To Destination**.
</Step>

<StepImage
  src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/6.png"
  alt="Work.ink final destination"
/>

<Step number={8} title="Return to Luarmor">
    You can now <a href="#final-steps">click here</a> to continue.
</Step>

## Final steps

<Step number={1} title="Generate or Renew Your Key">
    After being redirected back to **Luarmor**, click **Get a New Key**.

    If you already have a key, click **Renew** instead and skip to **Step 5**.
</Step>

<div className="flex flex-col md:flex-row gap-2 justify-center items-center">
    <StepImage
        src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/1.png"
        alt="Luarmor get new key"
        width={350}
        className="h-[250px] object-contain border-0"
    />

    <StepImage
        src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/renew.png"
        alt="Luarmor renew key"
        width={350}
        className="h-[250px] object-contain border-0"
    />
</div>

<Step number={2} title="Copy the Generated Key">
    Once the key is generated, copy it to your clipboard.
</Step>

<StepImage
    src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/2.png"
    alt="Copy key"
    width={500}
/>

<Step number={3} title="Redeem the Key in Discord">
    Open Discord and go to the [#get-script](https://discord.com/channels/1282361102935658777/1282373591652110417) channel in our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/mspaint).

    <Callout type="warn">
        If you can't find the channel, enable **Show All Channels** option in the server settings.

        <img width="200" height="200" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/allchannels.png" />
    </Callout>

    Click the **Redeem Key** button in the script panel.
</Step>

<StepImage
    src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/3.png"
    alt="Redeem key button"
    width={600}
/>

<Step number={4} title="Submit the Key">
    Paste the copied key into the input field and click **Submit**.

    <Callout type="warn">
        Ensure there are **no extra spaces** before or after the key, or you will receive the error:<br />
        **Something went wrong. Try again.**

        <img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/keycheck.png" />
    </Callout>
</Step>

<StepImage
    src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/4.png"
    alt="Submit key"
    width={500}
/>

<Step number={5} title="Get the Whitelisted Role">
    To unlock be able to **make suggestions, report bugs and access more channels**, you need to get the **Whitelisted** role.<br />

    Click the **Get Role** button in the script panel.
</Step>

<StepImage
    src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/5.png"
    alt="Get Whitelisted Role"
    width={500}
/>

<Step number={6} title="Get the Script">
    Click **Get Script**, scroll down and then copy and paste the provided script into your executor.
</Step>

<StepImage
    src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/6.png"
    alt="Get script"
    width={500}
/>
