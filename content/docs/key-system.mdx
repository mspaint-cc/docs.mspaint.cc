## Choosing a link shortener provider

- **Linkvertise**: 1 Checkpoint (12 hours key)
- **Work.ink**: 2 Checkpoints (8 hours key)

1. Go to the <a href="https://www.mspaint.cc/key">Key System Page</a> and select your checkpoint provider.

2. On the `Luarmor` page, select `Start`.
![help](/images/mspaint/keytutorial/start/1.png)

3. Continue at your selected provider: <a href="#through-linkvertise">Linkvertise</a> | <a href="#through-work-ink">work.ink</a>

### Through Linkvertise
1. Click the `Get mspaint key` button.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/linkvertise/3.png" />

2. Complete all required tasks, then click the `Get Access` button.

<Callout type="info">If you recieve a `one-hour` wait time, switch to <a href="#through-work-ink">work.ink</a> provider.</Callout>
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/linkvertise/4.png" />

3. Continue <a href="#final-steps">here</a>.

### Through work.ink
1. Click the `Go To Destination` button.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/1.png" />

2. Wait and then click the `Continue With Ads` button.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/2.png" />

3. Again click the `Go To Destination` button.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/3.png" />

4. Scroll down and then click the `Access Link` button, wait 10 seconds and click it again.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/4.png" />

5. Complete all required tasks.

<Callout type="info">You don't need to install anything from the required tasks, just click `Install` and wait until it's completed.</Callout>

6. Scroll down and then click the `Go To Destination` button.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/workink/6.png" />

7. Continue <a href="#final-steps">here</a>.

## Final steps
1. After being redirected back to `Luarmor`, click `Get a new key`.
<Callout type="info">
    If you already have a key, click the `Renew` button instead and skip to `step 5`.
    <img width="300" height="300" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/renew.png" />
</Callout>
<img width="400" height="400" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/1.png" />

2. Once the key is generated, copy it to your clipboard.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/2.png" />

3. Open Discord, go to the [#get-script](https://discord.com/channels/1282361102935658777/1282373591652110417) channel in our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/mspaint) and click the `Redeem Key` button.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/3.png" />

4. Paste the copied key into the text field and click `Submit`.
<Callout type="warn">
    Ensure there are no extra spaces before or after the key, otherwise you will get the `Something went wrong. Try again.` error.
    <img width="500" height="500" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/keycheck.png" />
</Callout>
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/4.png" />

5. Click the `Get Script` button and copy the provided script.
<img width="450" height="450" src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/5.png" />
<img src="/images/mspaint/keytutorial/finish/6.png" />
